About Site Name

Elysian: To Be Seen is a platform dedicated to showcasing contemporary art, photography, writing, and music. We believe in the power of creativity to transform perspectives and inspire new ways of seeing the world.

For submissions, join the Discord via the Submit button. Urgent enquiries can be made via the Contact page.

Submission Guidelines

  1. Send a short pitch (100-200 words) via the Contact page.
  2. Include 3-6 images (JPEG/PNG/WebP) or up to 3 pages of writing.
  3. State any prior publication/exhibition if relevant.


Editorial Notes

  • We prioritise clarity, originality, and attention to craft.
  • Submissions may receive light edits for length and clarity.
  • Credits and captions should be accurate and complete.
