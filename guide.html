Guide & Rules

Everything you need to know before submitting your work.

Submission Guidelines

  1. Send a short pitch (100-200 words) via the Contact page.
  2. Include 3-6 images (JPEG/PNG/WebP) or up to 3 pages of writing.
  3. State any prior publication or exhibition if relevant.

Editorial Notes

  • We prioritise clarity, originality, and attention to craft.
  • Submissions may receive light edits for length and clarity.
  • Credits and captions should be accurate and complete.
